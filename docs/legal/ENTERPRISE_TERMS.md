# QQueue Cloud Enterprise Terms

Effective Date: Draft - not yet effective

These Enterprise Terms supplement the QQueue Cloud Terms of Service for customers
on an enterprise plan or under a signed order form. Where these Enterprise Terms
conflict with the standard Terms of Service, these Enterprise Terms control for
the enterprise customer.

## 1. Scope

These terms apply where QQueue and the customer enter into an enterprise
agreement or order form referencing them. The order form specifies plan, fees,
term, usage entitlements, and any negotiated terms.

## 2. Subscription Term and Renewal

The subscription runs for the term stated in the order form. Unless otherwise
agreed, the term renews for successive periods of equal length unless either
party gives written notice of non-renewal before the end of the current term.

## 3. Fees and Payment

Fees, billing frequency, and payment terms are set out in the order form. Unless
stated otherwise, invoices are due within 30 days. Usage above the contracted
entitlement may be billed as overage at the rates in the order form.

## 4. Usage Entitlements

The order form defines included entitlements, such as monthly email volume,
contacts, seats, and API throughput. QQueue will notify the customer when usage
approaches contracted limits and will work in good faith on a plan adjustment
where appropriate.

## 5. Support and Service Levels

Enterprise customers receive the support tier and availability commitment stated
in the order form, which may reference or extend the QQueue Cloud SLA
(`docs/legal/SLA.md`).

## 6. Data Protection

The QQueue Cloud Data Processing Agreement
(`docs/legal/DATA_PROCESSING_AGREEMENT.md`) applies to enterprise customers.
Additional data protection, residency, or security commitments, if any, are set
out in the order form or an addendum.

## 7. Security and Compliance

QQueue maintains reasonable technical and organizational security measures.
Specific compliance commitments, audit rights, and security reviews, if any, are
defined in the order form or a separate addendum.

## 8. Confidentiality

Each party will protect the other's confidential information using at least
reasonable care and will use it only to perform under the agreement. This does
not apply to information that is public, independently developed, or lawfully
received from a third party.

## 9. Term, Suspension, and Termination

QQueue may suspend service for non-payment, abuse, security risk, or violation of
the Terms of Service, using commercially reasonable efforts to notify the
customer first where practicable. Termination rights and effects are as stated in
the order form and the Terms of Service.

## 10. Liability

Limitations of liability are as stated in the QQueue Cloud Terms of Service,
except where expressly modified in the order form.

## 11. Order of Precedence

In case of conflict, the order of precedence is: (1) the signed order form,
(2) these Enterprise Terms, (3) the QQueue Cloud Terms of Service, and
(4) other referenced policies.

## 12. Contact

Enterprise inquiries may be directed to:

support@qqueue.app
